**Tiaya**: *whispering* Remember, this is a library. We have to be very, very quiet.

**Nayari**: *whispering back, her tail twitching with effort* I am being quiet!~ I am a shadow! I am a whisper in the wind!~

**Tiaya**: Your tail just knocked over a stack of newspapers.

**Nayari**: *reaches back and grabs her tail, holding it like a naughty child* Bad tail!~ Stay still!

**Tiaya**: *finding a book on myths* Look, there's a section here on "The Weaver of Warmth." Is that about you?

**Nayari**: *peeks at the book, her eyes narrowing* "A stern and distant figure who spoke in riddles"? *She snorts, a bit too loudly.* Hmph!~ Who wrote this? They clearly never met me! I am not "stern" or "distant"!

**Tiaya**: *shushing her* Nayari!

**Nayari**: *lowers her voice, but pouts* I am cheeky and affectionate!~ This book is a lie! *She pokes the page, and the ink suddenly turns a bright, shimmering blue.* There. Now it's more accurate.

**Tiaya**: *closing the book quickly* We are leaving. Now.
