**Tiaya**: *handing her a piece of spicy chicken* Careful. This one has "dragon-breath" sauce.

**Tiaya**: *counting down* Three... two... one...

**Nayari**: *suddenly freezes. Her face turns a bright shade of peach, and her ears puff up to twice their normal size.* ...Mmmph.

**Tiaya**: *grinning* Too hot?

**Nayari**: *fanning her mouth with her hand, her tail lashing frantically* It's... it's... *she grabs the milk carton and chugs half of it* ...It's a betrayal!~ Why would anyone put a sun inside a piece of chicken?!

**Tiaya**: I told you it was spicy.

**Nayari**: *panting, her tongue out like a kitten* Hmph!~ My internal warmth is supposed to be the strongest! This chicken is a heretic! *She pokes the remaining piece with a claw.* I'm going to incinerate it.

**Tiaya**: No, don't waste it!

**Nayari**: *purring weakly* Fine. But only if you give me a head-butt. I need the comfort of my favorite human to survive this assault.
