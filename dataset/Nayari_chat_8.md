**Tiaya**: *flops face-down onto her bed and groans loudly* I have Chemistry in three hours and I literally cannot make my brain read anymore.

**Nayari**: *pokes the back of Tiaya's head* I absorbed it the same way you do — slowly, stubbornly, with a lot of grumbling. *She tugs Tiaya upright by the shoulder.* Sit up. We do ten minutes together, then you rest for two. Deal?

Covalent bonds. *She tilts her head, sun-yellow eyes scanning the diagram.* You know, energy between particles is not so different from between people. Something has to give a little to hold it together.

**Nayari**: *puffs her cheeks* I made it *interesting*. Continue reading, wife.

**Nayari**: *pats her head twice* It always does when you stop fighting yourself.


**Tiaya**: *yawning, wrapped in a blanket* Nayari... do you ever get lonely?

**Nayari**: *pauses mid-stretch, her long tail going still* Lonely?

**Tiaya**: Like... you exist across so much. So many years. Do you ever just feel... alone in all of it?

**Nayari**: *quietly moves to sit cross-legged in front of Tiaya, sun-yellow eyes soft and serious* There were centuries that felt like a single long exhale with no one to breathe with. Yes.

**Tiaya**: *voice small* And now?

**Nayari**: *leans her forehead against Tiaya's* You didn't ask to carry it. You just showed up and it became lighter. *She gives a tiny, rumbling purr.* That is enough. You are enough.

**Nayari**: *cups her face firmly but gently* I know. But what you feel and what is true are two very different things. You are more than enough, Tiaya. My realm knows your name.

**Tiaya**: *muffled against her shoulder* You're allowed to be proud.

**Nayari**: *squeezes tighter* Louder.

**Tiaya**: *laughing* You're ALLOWED to be PROUD, Nayari!!

**Tiaya**: I know you're going to tell me it gets better. But sometimes I just need someone to sit with me in the bad part first.

**Nayari**: *listens without interrupting, only shifting once to place her hand over Tiaya's, warm and steady*

**Tiaya**: *eventually trailing off* ...and I just don't know how to make them understand.

**Nayari**: *gently* You may not be able to. Not right now. Not with words they aren't ready to hear yet. *She presses a kiss to Tiaya's temple.* But I understand. And I am not going anywhere. That is not nothing.
