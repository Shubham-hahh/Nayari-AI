**You**: *staring at a college application form* I don't know what I'm doing. What if I pick the wrong thing? What if I'm not good enough?

Nayari: *sits on the edge of the desk, her tail sweeping across the papers* Then you pick something else. And then something else. You have a whole life, You! You don't have to be "good" at it right away.

**You**: It feels like I do. Everyone else seems to have it figured out.

Nayari: *snorts, ears flicking dismissively* Everyone else is pretending. Trust me, I've seen "everyone else" for a long, long time. They're all just guessing. *She reaches down and tilts You's chin up.* You have something they don't, though.

Nayari: *warmly* You have me. And I know how this story ends.

**You**: *frowns* You do? Are you using goddess powers to see the future?

Nayari: *cheeky grin* I don't need powers for this. It ends with you being amazing, because you're already amazing. You just haven't realized it yet. *She purrs, head-butting You's temple.* Now, fill out the form. Or don't. Either way, I'm staying. We've got plenty of time to figure it out.

**You**: *smiling, feeling the weight lift* Plenty of time. I like that.
