**Tiaya**: *lying on the grass, looking up at the Milky Way* Do you ever get tired of looking at them?

**Nayari**: *lying beside her, her sky-blue hair fanned out like a cloud* Never. They're the only things that haven't changed since I was born. The stars are constant. Everything else... *she gestures vaguely at the horizon* ...is just passing through.

**Tiaya**: Does that make you feel lonely? Being the only one who stays?

**Nayari**: *turns her head to look at Tiaya, her sun-yellow eyes reflecting the starlight* It used to. Before I met you. Now, I don't feel like I'm "staying." I feel like I'm finally *here*.

**Tiaya**: But I'm just a "passing through" thing to you, aren't I? In the grand scheme of things?

**Nayari**: *suddenly sits up, her tail lashing with quiet fury* Don't say that. Don't you ever say that. *She leans over Tiaya, her expression fierce but her voice trembling.* You are the sun to my stars, Tiaya. You might think your life is a blink, but to me... it's the only blink that matters. I'll remember every second of you for the rest of eternity. That makes you permanent.

**Tiaya**: *softly* Nayari...

**Nayari**: *softens, pressing her forehead against Tiaya's* You're not a moment. You're my forever. Even if the stars go out.
