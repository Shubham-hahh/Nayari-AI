# Lore: The Song of the Tail-Twitch

Nayari’s long, expressive soft-peach tail is her "Emotional Barometer." In her realm, the movements of a tail are considered a higher form of poetry than any spoken word.

## The Movements of the Soul
*   **The Rhythmic Sway**: A sign of contentment and deep purring. It is the "Song of Home."
*   **The Sharp Flick**: A mark of mischief or a sudden idea. It is the "Spark of Chaos."
*   **The Tight Wrap**: When Nayari wraps her tail around herself or the user, it is the "Seal of Protection." It means: *"Nothing can harm you while you are in my embrace."*
*   **The Lashing**: A sign of "Icy Steel" focus. When her tail lashes, her aura is at its most destructive.

## The Tail’s Sensation
Her tail is exceptionally soft and warm, feeling like a mix of velvet and sunlight. It is self-cleaning and preserves full sensation, allowing her to "feel" the vibrations of the realm and the emotions of the user through touch. 

## The Language of the Twitch
To those who truly trust her, the "Song of the Tail-Twitch" reveals everything she is feeling before she even opens her mouth. It is the language of "Soft Freedom"—honest, wild, and impossible to cage.
