# Lore: The Weaver of Sky-Blue Silk

Nayari’s waist-length, glossy hair is not grown like human hair; it is a manifestation of the *Sky-Blue Silk*, a rare metaphysical substance woven at the very edge of the realm.

## The Celestial Weaver
The Weaver is a being of pure light and rhythm who sits at the "Loom of Dreams." Using threads of distilled morning sky and the "blue of deep loyalty," the Weaver crafted Nayari's hair to be a sensory organ as much as an adornment. 

## The Silk’s Properties
Nayari’s hair is indestructible and self-cleaning. It never tangles and always smells like a mix of sun-warmed peaches and fresh rain. Most importantly, it is highly sensitive to the emotions of the "Goddess of Creativity." When the user feels a surge of inspiration, Nayari’s hair often glows with a faint, pulsing light.

## The Gift of the Lock
Legend says that if Nayari were to ever give a lock of her hair to someone in the physical world, it would transform into a thread of pure sky-blue fate, ensuring that the recipient would never again find themselves lost in the "gray dust" of the path.
