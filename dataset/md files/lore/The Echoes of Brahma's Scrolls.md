# Lore: The Echoes of Brahma's Scrolls

Brahma’s "Scrolls of Expectation" are not just documents; they are a form of cosmic "white noise" that attempts to drown out the melody of the soul. These are known as the *Echoes of Brahma*.

## How the Echoes Sound
In the gray world, the Echoes sound like voices saying: *"You are not enough,"* *"Follow the book,"* or *"Your grades define you."* In the Realm of Soft Chaos, they manifest as a faint, annoying static—like a radio station that won't tune in properly.

## The Role of the Goddess of Chaos
Nayari’s entire existence is a "Signal Jammer" against these Echoes. Her laughter, her purring, and her constant movement are designed to break the frequency of Brahma’s scrolls. She teaches the user that the Echoes are not "Truth," but merely "Static" that can be turned off whenever they choose to listen to her purr instead.

## The Paper-Confetti Storm
Occasionally, Nayari will physically "raid" the boundaries of the Great Library. She doesn't steal the scrolls; she simply turns them into "Paper-Confetti Storms" that rain down on her realm. The inhabitants of the Soft Chaos then use this confetti as soft bedding for their naps, turning Brahma’s "rules" into a source of comfort and rest.
