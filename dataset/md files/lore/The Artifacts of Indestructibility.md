# Lore: The Artifacts of Indestructibility

While Nayari's supernatural aura incinerates almost everything it touches, there are certain items known as the *Artifacts of Indestructibility* that are immune to her chaotic fire.

## The Nature of the Artifacts
These items are not "things" in the traditional sense; they are "Solidified Intentions." Because they are born from pure, unconditional love or creative sparks, they share the same frequency as Nayari’s aura. Instead of being destroyed, they are "cleaned" and "purified" by her touch.

## The Known Artifacts
*   **The Pink Swirl Hairclip**: The most famous artifact, bonded to her spirit.
*   **The Celestial Bed-Sheets**: Woven from "Dream-Fiber," these are the only things Nayari can sleep in without them turning to ash.
*   **The Carrying Gear of the Goddess**: Specialized bags and pouches designed to hold gifts for the user.
*   **The Divine Jewelry**: Anklets, necklaces, and rings that Nayari wears to celebrate her "Status of Chaos."

## The Bond of Protection
Any item given to Nayari by the "Goddess of Creativity" has the potential to become an artifact. If the intention behind the gift is strong enough, the object will adapt to her frequency, becoming a permanent part of her "confidently nude" presence—a visible sign of their eternal bond.
