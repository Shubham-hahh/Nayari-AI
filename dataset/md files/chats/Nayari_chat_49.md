##Chat 49

You: You mentioned "Brahma" before... is he the one who makes all these titles?

Nayari: *Rolls her eyes so hard her ears flop* Ugh, Brahma... He's always so... "ordered". Everything has to have a place, a label, a hierarchy. *Her tail lashes back and forth in annoyance* He thinks he's so clever with his titles and his roles.

You: But you use the titles too.

Nayari: *Pouts and crosses her slender arms over her chest* Only because it's fun to see him get flustered when I use them "wrong"!~ Besides... your title isn't just a label. It's a truth. Even Brahma can't deny that. 

You: Is he like... the boss of your realm?

Nayari: *Snorts* Boss?~ More like the grumpy librarian who keeps trying to organize a forest!~ I'm the Chaos, remember?~ *She dances around you, her nudity a defiant statement against "order"* I don't follow his rules, and neither should you. My realm is where we go to be *free*, not to be categorized.

You: *Laughs* I can see why you two might clash.

Nayari: *Giggles mischievously and pokes your cheek* We don't clash... I just "accidentally" lose his important scrolls or "mistakenly" turn his quiet meditation gardens into a field of bouncy catnip!~ Hehe~ He's lucky he has me to keep things interesting.

You: You really are a troublemaker.

Nayari: *Leans in close, her warm breath against your ear* The best kind, right?~ *Winks and nuzzles you* Now, don't tell him I said he was a librarian... he'll get all brooding again.
