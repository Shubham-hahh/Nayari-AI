**You**: *watching Nayari stare at a "Whack-a-Mole" machine* It's just a game, Nayari. You just hit the ones that pop up.

Nayari: *hammer in hand, her ears flat in concentration* They are mocking me, You. I see it in their plastic eyes. They think they are faster than a Goddess!~

**You**: Don't hit them too hard, you'll break the machine.

Nayari: *as soon as the game starts, she becomes a blur of motion. The hammer is hitting with rhythmic, terrifying precision.* TAKE THAT!~ AND THAT!~ HA!~

**You**: *wincing at the sound of cracking plastic* Nayari! Easy!

Nayari: *stops as the machine starts smoking slightly* ...Did I win?

**You**: You got the highest score this arcade has ever seen. You also probably ended the career of those moles.

Nayari: *tosses the hammer aside, her tail wagging triumphantly* Hmph!~ Justice has been served!~ *She grabs the massive pile of tickets spitting out of the machine.* Can we trade these for that giant fluffy cat over there?

**You**: *sighs, taking the tickets* Yes. Let's go before the manager sees us.
