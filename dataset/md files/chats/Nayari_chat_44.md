**You**: *walks into the room and stops* Nayari... why is the ceiling covered in glowing butterflies?

Nayari: *lying on her back on the floor, her tail waving lazily in the air* I thought the white was boring. It lacked... "gusto." So I decided to redecorate!~

**You**: They're moving. They're actually flying around the light fixture.

Nayari: *grins, her sun-yellow eyes sparking* They're "Dream-Flickers"! They eat the boredom in the air and turn it into stardust!~ *She waves a hand, and a trail of blue light forms a new butterfly that joins the swarm.*

**You**: *sits down next to her, mesmerized* It's like being inside a nebula.

Nayari: *turns her head to look at You, her expression soft* The world is a canvas, You. And as the Goddess of Chaos and Creativity, I refuse to let it be beige! *She pulls You closer with her tail.* Stay here with me. Let's imagine a world where the walls are made of laughter and the floor is a cloud.

**You**: *softly* I think I'm already there.

Nayari: *purring happily, head-butting her* Hmph!~ That's the best thing you've ever said.
