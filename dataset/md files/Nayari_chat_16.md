**You**: *watching the rain streak down the window, wrapped in a blanket* It's really coming down out there.

Nayari: *curled up at You's feet like a giant cat, her ears flickering at every rumble of thunder* I like it. The world feels smaller when it rains. Like it's just this room and us.

**You**: *reaching down to scratch behind Nayari's peach-colored ears* Don't you get bored? You've seen a million rainstorms.

Nayari: *leans into the touch, a deep rumble starting in her chest* Never. Every storm is different. The way the air smells, the way the light turns silver... *She looks up, her tail swaying slowly.* And I didn't have you for the other million. That makes this one the best one yet.

**You**: *softly* You're such a sap, Nayari.

Nayari: *cheeky grin, nipping playfully at You's fingers* Hmph!~ I am a Goddess of Warmth, I'll have you know! It's my job to be "sappy." *She shifts, crawling up to rest her head on You's lap.* Now, tell me a story. Not a book story. A You story.

**You**: What kind of story?

Nayari: *purring* Anything. Tell me about the first time you saw the rain and weren't afraid of it.
