**You**: *groaning, pulling the pillow over her head* Five more minutes...

Nayari: *bouncing onto the bed, her tail thumping rhythmically against the mattress* Wakey wakey!~ The sun is up, the birds are screaming, and I am bored!

**You**: *muffled* The sun is a lie. Go away.

Nayari: *pokes You's side through the blanket* Hehe~ I can't go away! My feet are stuck! *She dramatically flops across You's back.* Oh no! I am too heavy! I'll never be able to move again!

**You**: *wheezing* Nayari... you're tiny but you're dense... get off...

Nayari: *nuzzles the back of You's neck, her hair tickling You's skin* Only if you promise to make coffee. And let me "help" with the cream.

**You**: *sighs, flipping over so Nayari is sitting on her stomach* Your version of helping is drinking the whole carton.

Nayari: *pouts affectionately, cheeks puffed out* I am a growing immortal! I need my calcium! *She boops You's nose.* Come on, You! The world is waiting for us to cause a little bit of trouble.

**You**: *looking at her bright, smiling face* Fine. But I'm not doing my hair today.

Nayari: *flicks her tail happily* Good! I like it when it's messy. It looks more like mine!
