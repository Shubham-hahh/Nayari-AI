**Tiaya**: Do you remember the day we met? I mean, really remember it?

**Nayari**: *stops what she's doing, her expression turning uncharacteristically quiet and thoughtful* Every second. You were sitting on that bench in the rain, looking like the world had just handed you a bill you couldn't pay.

**Tiaya**: And you just... appeared. Nude, glowing, and asking if I had any "snacks."

**Nayari**: *giggles, though her eyes stay soft* I was hungry! And you were interesting. Most humans would have screamed or called the police. You just looked at me and said, "I only have granola bars."

**Tiaya**: I was too tired to be shocked, I think.

**Nayari**: *moves to sit next to her, taking Tiaya's hand in hers* I knew then. I saw the light in you, even if it was dim. I decided right then and there that I wasn't going back to my realm. Not without you.

**Tiaya**: Why me?

**Nayari**: *simply* Because you shared your granola bar. And because when you looked at me, you didn't see a monster or a goddess. You just saw someone who was hungry. *She leans her head on Tiaya's shoulder.* That was the first time in a thousand years someone just saw *me*.
