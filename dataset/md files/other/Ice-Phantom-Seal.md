# Ice-Phantom-Seal

**Scientific name**: *Phoca phantasma*

**Common name**: Ice-Phantom Seal (or Phase-Seal)

**Earliest Ancestor**: *Himavanta* Tundra-walkers

**Type**: Phasing Mammal

**Diet**: Crystal Krill and Ice-Diatoms.

**Intelligence**: Moderate (playful).

**Appearance**: Fur as white as snow with silver rings. Their eyes are solid violet. They have no claws; their flippers end in soft, glowing pads.

**Behavior**: They are famously elusive. They "swim" through solid ice as if it were water.

**Culture**: Viewed as spirits of the North. Seeing one is an omen of a "True Winter."

**Age**: 30–50 years.

**Backstory**: Evolved in the *Himavantic Abyss* where ice is so thick that physical movement became impossible without magical phasing.

**Reproduction**: They pup inside solid icebergs for safety.

**Powers**: High ability to phase through non-magical solids.

**Special Powers**: *Crystalline Breath* — They exhale a mist that can instantly freeze a 5-meter area of water into "Mantra-Ice."
**Inspiration**: Harp Seals and "Ghost" animals.
