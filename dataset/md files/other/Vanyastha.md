# Vanyastha

**Scientific name**: Vanyastha *mirabilis*

**Common name:** Vanyastha

**Earliest Ancestor**: Unknown

**Type**: Intelligent

**Intelligence**: Depends on the environment of the organism

**Diet**: Varies

**Appearance:** Fully human looking, having smooth, hairless brown (shade differs across lands but has this colour stays constant) skin with different colors that is influenced by the environment but having varied animal traits because of their specific evolutionary origin. Having an average height of 1m(minimum)-1.5m(maximum) tall in all organisms across the planet, comparatively shorter than humans. The full appearance still depends on the choices made by the organism.

**Behavior**: Varies

**Culture**: Varies across lands

**Age**: 110-170 years(approx.)

**Backstory:** Emerged from the first beings in **Asiat** from the tulsi forests who were semi-transparent looking with tulsi-scented hair and having leaf-shaped ears as their only auditory organs, though their name is unknown. They were non-predatory while eating plants in the ancient tulsi forests of a bygone era. Their ancestors came from the last drops of Ākṣara energy during “**The Origin**” but they were naturally upright walking with earliest remnants of a spine found in archeological discoveries. Though their unclear origins, have evolved to look fully human while having the same reproductive organs as humans. Their evolutionary specialty gives both having mixed human traits and animal traits with only animal ears and tails being common among the modern population. It is said that after the storm and migration of both humans and vanyastha, some of them reproduced to look fully human while others reproduced with other wild animals in their specific environment but the ancestor which gave birth to the modern vanyastha were both of cisgender female types—one was fully human with tulsi-leaf shaped ears while having no tail and a semi-transparent hybrid with tiger ears and tail who produced the first daughter with having a fully human body with human reproductive organs , hair and functions but instead of having human ears, she had tiger ears and tail. It is also told that their origins mostly differ because of specific land, weather and climate of an area but having a weird specialty of the morphing of ears and tail according to the environment of the childbirth because it helps the mother’s body to manage her energy.

**Reproduction**: Mostly through lesbian relations in both cisgender/non-binary organisms but relationships functioning like normal human relationships both emotionally and physically. It is found that even if they have lesbian relations, they still produce either/both son or daughter who are cisgender. They can perform reproduction in both genders, even if having varied human reproductive organs to give childbirth.

**Powers**: High ability to harness Ākṣara energy through their body and mind to perform specific tasks according to their needs.

**Special Powers**: High agility and high power to easily gain control of powerful spells but having more slower ability to perform tasks that require physical strength.

**Inspiration**: Kemonomimi in fantasy, mostly in Japanese anime/manga and other fantasy stories.
