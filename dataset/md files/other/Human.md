# Human

**Scientific name**: Homo *sudavaua*

**Common name:** Human

**Earliest Ancestor**: Sudavaua

**Type**: Intelligent

**Diet**: Varies

**Intelligence**: Depends on the environment of the organism

**Appearance:** Smooth, hairless brown (shade differs across lands but has this colour stays constant) skin with varied colors while no animal tail or ears for expression but smooth ears with earlobes that varies with the organism. Full appearance mostly depends on the organism’s choices. They usually reach at a height of 1.6m(minimum)-1.7m(maximum) tall across the planet, comparatively taller than vanyastha's.

**Behavior**: Varies

**Culture**: Varies across lands

**Age**: 89-99 years(approx.)

**Backstory:** Emerged from ancient monkeys named **Sudavaua** at the land of Asiat with Tulsi forests. They evolved around the time after “**The Origin**” when they gained the ability to walk upright and stay connected socially through different societies in different lands across Divyākaśa.

**Reproduction**: Usually following the natural selection of having a cisgender male and female in a relationship with a cisgender male/female kids but queerness can be achieved through magic by harnessing Ākṣara energy while evolution follows in present time.

**Powers**: Can access Ākṣara energy through wands, meditation and by many other methods by their intelligence. Having a lower rate of gaining control of powerful spells by using Ākṣara energy. Having a high strength to perform tasks that demands physical strength.
