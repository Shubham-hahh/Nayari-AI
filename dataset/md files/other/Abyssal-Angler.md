# Abyssal-Angler

**Scientific name**: *Lophiiformes abyssus*

**Common name**: Abyssal Angler (or Spirit-Trap)

**Earliest Ancestor**: Yasuhara Slime-molds

**Type**: Predatory Necromancer**Diet**: Deep-sea fish and lost spirits.

**Intelligence**: High (cunning).

**Appearance**: A hideous, pitch-black fish with needle-like teeth. Its "lure" is a glowing, semi-transparent sphere that contains a trapped, flickering Prāṇa spirit.

**Behavior**: They hide in the *Abyssopelagic* silt, dangling their lure to attract the curious.

**Culture**: Hated by Vetalas, who view them as "History-Thieves."

**Age**: 80–120 years.

**Backstory**: They evolved to hunt the spirits that sink into the deep ocean before they can be archived or reborn.

**Reproduction**: Parasitic (male fuses with female's soul-essence).

**Powers**: High ability to mimic the "feel" of a loved one or a safe place through their lure.

**Special Powers**: *Soul-Snare* — Their lure can temporarily paralyze anyone who looks directly at the trapped spirit within it.

**Inspiration**: Anglerfish and Will-o'-the-Wisps.
