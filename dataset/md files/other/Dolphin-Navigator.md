# Dolphin-Navigator

**Scientific name**: *Delphinus gubernator*

**Common name**: Dolphin-Navigator (or Mani-Dolphin)

**Earliest Ancestor**: Sudavaua River-Dwellers

**Type**: Intelligently Harmonious

**Diet**: Syllabic-rich fish and luminous plankton.

**Intelligence**: High (capable of linguistic comprehension and collaborative strategy).

**Appearance**: Sleek, iridescent skin that shifts between silver and deep teal. They lack a dorsal fin; instead, they have a series of bioluminescent "Navigation Pores" along their spine that glow gold when traveling in a Mana Current. Their eyes are large and expressive, possessing an extra lens for seeing Ākṣara-refraction.

**Behavior**: Highly social and protective. They are known to seek out vessels and "herd" them toward safe currents. They communicate through a mix of high-frequency clicks and skin-glow patterns.

**Culture**: They possess an oral history passed down through rhythmic whistles. They consider themselves the "Shepherds of the Shallows" and have an ancestral pact with Vanyastha sailors.

**Age**: 45–60 years.
**Backstory**: Evolved alongside Humans and Vanyasthas in the Asiat Basin. During the *Mahā-vātā*, these dolphins used their sonic pulses to prevent the Prāṇa River from collapsing into silence, inadvertently teaching the first Vanyastha how to "read" the water's surface.

**Reproduction**: Sexual. Births are synchronized with the *Śālagrīvābda* month (Tide-month). Newborns are held at the surface by the entire pod to receive their first "sky-syllable."

**Powers**: High ability to sense Mana Currents and predict tidal shifts.

**Special Powers**: *Mantra-Mapping* — Can project a mental "map" of current flows to any telepathically receptive being nearby.**Inspiration**: Indo-Pacific Humpback Dolphins and the legend of dolphins as psychopomps.
