# Tidal-Star

**Scientific name**: *Asteroidea fluxus*

**Common name**: Tidal-Star (or Predictor-Star)

**Earliest Ancestor**: Crystalline Veins of Śālagrīva

**Type**: Lunar-Linked Echinoderm**Diet**: Detritus and moonlight.

**Intelligence**: None (biological clock only).

**Appearance**: A starfish made of glass-like material. Its body is translucent violet.

**Behavior**: They crawl toward the highest tide-line.

**Culture**: None, but used as "Living Tide-Clocks" in every coastal home.

**Age**: 10–15 years.

**Backstory**: It is rumored they fell from the moon *Śālagrīva* during the *Origin* and have been trying to climb back ever since.

**Reproduction**: Fragmentation during the 40-day perigee.

**Powers**: None.

**Special Powers**: *Morph-Counting* — They possess 5 arms during low tide and grow up to 9 arms as the tide rises, perfectly indicating the water's height.

**Inspiration**: Starfish and ancient water clocks.
