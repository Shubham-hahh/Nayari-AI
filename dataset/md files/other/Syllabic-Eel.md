# Syllabic-Eel

**Scientific name**: *Anguilla vidyut*

**Common name**: Syllabic Eel (or Bolt-Tongue)

**Earliest Ancestor**: *Ākṣara-Vent Flora*

**Type**: Bio-Electrical Conduit

**Diet**: Mineral-rich silt and electrical storms.

**Intelligence**: Low (Primal).

**Appearance**: Long, ribbon-like body (4m) that appears to be made of liquid mercury. Sparks of violet Ākṣara jump between its scales.

**Behavior**: They live in the *Mid-Oceanic Ridges*, wrapping themselves around the crystalline stems of vent-flora to "recharge."

**Culture**: Their skins are used by Human mages to craft the most powerful lightning-wands.

**Age**: 60–90 years.

**Backstory**: They were created when a lightning strike from a *Teja-stambha* flare hit an open Ākṣara-vent, fusing the celestial and core energies.

**Reproduction**: They swim into the *Thermosphere* during mana-storms to spawn in the golden clouds.

**Powers**: High electrical discharge and current-conduction.

**Special Powers**: *Mouna-Jhatkā* (Silent Shock) — Their electrical discharge silences the "internal voice" of a mage, making them unable to remember any spells for one hour.\\

**Inspiration**: Electric Eels and lightning.
