# Armor-Scribe-Turtle

**Scientific name**: *Chelonia scripta*

**Common name**: Armor-Scribe Turtle (or Law-Bearer)

**Earliest Ancestor**: Lithic Scrub of the Asiat Basin

**Type**: Long-Lived Chronicler

**Diet**: *Mantra-Kelp* and calcified syllables.

**Intelligence**: High (Possesses "Deep Time" awareness).

**Appearance**: Massive sea turtles with shells resembling weathered temple stone. The scutes of their shells are naturally etched with the "Common Law" of the sea. Their skin is leathery and etched with wrinkles that look like ancient maps.

**Behavior**: Extremely slow and non-aggressive. They spend decades floating in the *Primary Gyre*, acting as living buoys for other lifeforms.

**Culture**: They are the "Lords of Precedent." When a legal dispute occurs in Brahmapurī, judges often wait for a Turtle to drift near the shore; the character etched on its newest scute is considered the final verdict.

**Age**: 800–1,200 years.

**Backstory**: They were the first creatures to crawl upon the *Rivasaga* stone when land first rose. They carry the "weight of the world" literally and figuratively.

**Reproduction**: Once every century, they return to the *Sudavaua Hills* to bury eggs in mantra-sand.

**Powers**: Immunity to all forms of temporal distortion and memory-erasure.

**Special Powers**: *Dharma-Bhāra* (The Weight of Law) — They can increase their density to become unmovable by any current or spell, anchoring local reality during mana-quakes.

**Inspiration**: The World Turtle (Akupara) and ancient steles.
