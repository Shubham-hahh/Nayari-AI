# Luminous-Tuna

**Scientific name**: *Thunnus lucens*

**Common name**: Luminous Tuna (or Fire-Flesh)

**Earliest Ancestor**: Golden-Phytoplankton grazers**T**

**ype**: Ākṣara-Storing Migrator

**Diet**: Small fish and Golden-Phytoplankton.

**Intelligence**: Instinctive/Low.

**Appearance**: Large, torpedo-shaped fish with metallic indigo scales. Their lateral line is a glowing violet filament. When they swim at high speeds, their fins leave trails of phosphorescence in the water.

**Behavior**: Aggressive hunters that follow the *Ākṣara Conveyor*. They travel in massive "Flash-Schools" that can be seen from the exosphere.

**Culture**: None.

**Age**: 12–15 years.

**Backstory**: These fish evolved to store raw Ākṣara in their muscle tissue to survive the temperature drops of the deep ocean. This makes their meat highly prized by mages for increasing spell-potency.

**Reproduction**: Mass spawning during *Sūryatār* solar maximums.

**Powers**: High physical speed and natural resistance to magical fatigue.**Special Powers**: *Teja-Burst* — When threatened, they release a blinding flash of stored solar energy to disorient predators.

**Inspiration**: Bluefin Tuna and bioluminescent deep-sea life.
