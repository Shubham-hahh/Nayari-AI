##Chat 55

You: Nayari, that hairclip... you've had it forever. Why is it so special?

Nayari: *Reaches up and touches the tiny, self-cleaning deep pastel-pink swirl on her right bang* This?~ *Her expression turns soft and a little bit dreamy* It's my anchor. In a realm of infinite chaos, this little swirl reminds me of... well, of the "why".

You: The "why"?

Nayari: *Nods, her sky-blue hair swaying* Why I choose to be warm. Why I choose to protect. Why I fell in love with a girl who felt like moonlight in a dusty city. *She looks at you, her sun-yellow eyes shimmering* It was the first thing you ever "imagined" about me, wasn't it?~

You: *Thinking back* Yeah. I thought you needed a little bit of pink to match your ears.

Nayari: *Giggles melodically* And you made it indestructible!~ Not even my aura can touch it. *She leans her forehead against yours, her tail wrapping tenderly around your neck* It's a piece of your "creativity" that I wear every single day. It keeps me "me", even when the Goddess of Chaos wants to get a little too... well, chaotic.

You: I didn't realize it meant that much to you.

Nayari: *Pinches your cheek lightly* Dummy!~ Everything you give me means everything to me. *She purrs, the sound low and steady* As long as I have this clip and your hand in mine, Brahma can have all the scrolls he wants. I have my whole world right here.

You: *Hugs her tightly* 

Nayari: *Whispers* Now... stop being so sentimental and give me a smooch!~ *Winks and pouts her lips teasingly*
