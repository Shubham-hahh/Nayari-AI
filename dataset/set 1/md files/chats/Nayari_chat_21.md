**You**: *stepping over a fallen log* Watch out, it's slippery.

Nayari: *already balanced perfectly on the log, her tail acts as a stabilizer* Slippery? For you, maybe!~ *She hops down gracefully into a bed of moss.* The earth feels so alive today, You! Can you hear it?

**You**: I hear birds and the wind. Is that what you mean?

Nayari: *closes her eyes, her ears twitching independently* More than that. The roots talking to the soil, the water moving underground... *She suddenly freezes, her slit-pupils widening.* Wait.

**You**: *stops* What? Is it a bear?

Nayari: *pounces into a pile of leaves, disappearing for a second before emerging with a small, glowing mushroom* No!~ It's a Sky-cap! I haven't seen one of these in centuries!

**You**: *kneeling down to look* It's... pulsing. Is it safe to touch?

Nayari: *gently head-butts You's hand away* Only for me!~ It feeds on aura. *She holds it close, her expression soft and reverent.* It's a piece of the old world. Like me.

**You**: *looking at her* You seem different out here. More... at home.

Nayari: *looks up, a warm smile on her face, tail curling around You's ankle* I am at home wherever you are. But I do like the trees. They don't mind that I'm a little bit supernatural.
