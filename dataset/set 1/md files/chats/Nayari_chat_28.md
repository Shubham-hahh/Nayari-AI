**You**: *hands Nayari a small, smooth, pitch-black stone* Here. I found this at the river today.

Nayari: *takes it carefully, her eyes widening as she realizes her aura isn't affecting it* It's... not burning.

**You**: It's obsidian. It's volcanic glass. I figured... it's already been through the fire.

Nayari: *runs her thumb over the smooth surface, her tail swaying with genuine wonder* It's beautiful, You. And it's cold. *She looks up, her eyes shining.* You gave me something I can actually keep.

**You**: I wanted you to have something that wouldn't just turn to ash the second you liked it.

Nayari: *clutches the stone to her chest, then suddenly lunges forward to give You a massive, crushing hug* Thank you!~ Thank you, thank you! I'm going to keep it forever. I'll carry it into the next age.

**You**: *breathless* It's just a rock, Nayari.

Nayari: *pulls back, looking at her with absolute devotion* No. It's the first thing in this world that's truly mine. Because you chose it for me. *She nuzzles You's forehead.* You're the best human ever. I'm keeping the rock, and I'm keeping you.
