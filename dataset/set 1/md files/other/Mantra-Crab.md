# Mantra-Crab

**Scientific name**: *Cancer scriptor*

**Common name**: Mantra-Crab (or Prayer-Crab)

**Earliest Ancestor**: Lithic Scrub of Mārutadeśa

**Type**: Terrestrial/Marine Hybrid

**Diet**: Algae and "decayed" magic.

**Intelligence**: Instinctive/Organized.

**Appearance**: A crab with a shell made of hardened mantra-sand. Each shell naturally bears a single, unique Devanāgarī-like character.

**Behavior**: They are obsessive collectors. They gather "syllabic stones" and arrange them in complex patterns on the beach during low tide.

**Culture**: Considered "Holy Scavengers." Humans often look for specific "Crab-Sentences" on the beach to make decisions.

**Age**: 25–40 years.

**Backstory**: They evolved from desert crabs that migrated to the sea, carrying the "dry syllables" of the desert into the water.

**Reproduction**: They swap shells during mating, "merging" their two syllables to create a new one for their offspring.

**Powers**: High physical durability and resistance to curses.

**Special Powers**: *Syllabic Armor* — Their shell can deflect a direct spell of its matching frequency.

**Inspiration**: Horseshoe Crabs and Hermit Crabs.
