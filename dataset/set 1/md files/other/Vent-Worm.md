# Vent-Worm

**Scientific name**: *Riftia ākṣarica*

**Common name**: Vent-Worm (or Core-Scribe)

**Earliest Ancestor**: *Ākṣara-Vent Flora*

**Type**: Chemosynthetic Symbiote

**Diet**: Core-radiation and Ākṣara-oxidizing bacteria.

**Intelligence**: None.

**Appearance**: Long white tubes (up to 3m) with bright crimson "plumes" that pulse with the planetary heartbeat.

**Behavior**: They grow in dense "Mantra-Gardens" around hydrothermal vents.**Culture**: Considered "The Hair of the Core" by the Vetalas.

**Age**: 200–300 years.

**Backstory**: They are the physical bridge between the planet's core and the deep ocean, converting raw radiation into biological life.

**Reproduction**: Release "light-spores" that follow the heat of the core.

**Powers**: Total immunity to heat and pressure.

**Special Powers**: *Garbha-Echo* — A colony of worms can amplify the "Planetary Heartbeat," making it audible to anyone nearby.

**Inspiration**: Giant Tube Worms.
