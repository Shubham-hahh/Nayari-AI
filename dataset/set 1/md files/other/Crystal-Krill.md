# Crystal-Krill

**Scientific name**: *Euphausia crystallina*

**Common name**: Crystal Krill (or Spark-Shrimp)

**Earliest Ancestor**: *Ākṣara-Diatoms*

**Type**: Base Mana-Consumer

**Diet**: Diatoms and light.

**Intelligence**: None.

**Appearance**: Tiny (2cm), perfectly transparent shrimp that look like shards of glass. When they eat, you can see the golden magic moving through their gut.

**Behavior**: They form swarms so large they can be seen from space as "Mana-Clouds."

**Culture**: None, but they are the most important animal on the planet—without them, the Whales would starve and reality would collapse.

**Age**: 1–2 years.

**Backstory**: They are the primary converters of raw *Sūryatār* light into animal protein.

**Reproduction**: Billions of eggs released during the *Prāṇotsava* (Year-Start).\\

**Powers**: None.

**Special Powers**: *Collective Glow* — A swarm can synchronize their pulses to create a "False Sun," tricking predators into swimming the wrong way.

**Inspiration**: Antarctic Krill.
