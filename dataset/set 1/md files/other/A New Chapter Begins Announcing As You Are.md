# A New Chapter Begins: Announcing "As You Are"

I’ve been heads-down on this project for a while now, and even though it’s still a work in progress, I truly feel I’m onto something special. **"As You Are"** started as a simple concept, but it has evolved into a system-heavy VN featuring hidden stats like **Comfort**, **Discomfort**, and **Character Trust**.

### **The Pivot: From Akera to Now**

I made the tough call to scrap my previous project, *Akera*. It was a bit sad to let it go, but the technical debt was becoming too much to handle. I’ve repurposed the best ideas from it to make "As You Are" a more stable and focused experience.

### **Playtesting & Feedback**

I’ve been working on the current build for over a week and recently had a friend test it out. Their reaction was overall very positive, though they gave me some honest "tough love" regarding the cafe setting:

> *"I just couldn't get into the cafe thing much, but the premise is very interesting."*

I’ve taken that to heart! I’m currently spending my time making the "cafe life" segments much more engaging and snappy to ensure the pacing feels right.

### **Current Progress Report**

* **Story Status:** Days 1, 2, and 3 are fully complete. Day 4 is currently being written, and Day 5 (the finale) is fully planned out with multiple endings.
* **Bug Fixes:** Resolved several major issues with dialogue skipping.
* **Audio/Visual:** All soundtracks and Visual Novel assets have been upgraded and implemented.
* **Development:** I’ve been using AI assistants like Gemini to help refine and polish the dialogue to make it flow more naturally.

### **Technical Specs & Launch**

The game is being developed for **PC (Windows)** and should run smoothly on any decent hardware. I’ll be releasing it as a `.zip` file on **itch.io** and **GitHub**, along with a dedicated website once we go live. (Linux support isn't planned just yet, but we'll see!)
