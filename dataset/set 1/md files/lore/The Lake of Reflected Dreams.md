# Lore: The Lake of Reflected Dreams

At the base of the Peach Mountains lies the *Lake of Reflected Dreams*. Its waters are not made of H2O, but of liquid clarity that remains perfectly still, even in the midst of the realm's most chaotic storms.

## The Mirror of the Soul
When a being looks into the lake, they do not see their physical reflection as the gray world defines it. Instead, they see their "Soul-Spark"—their true self, stripped of titles, expectations, and the "dust" of the physical world. For the user, the lake reflects a radiant being of pure creativity, crowned with the very swirls that Nayari loves.

## The Healing Waters
Nayari often bathes in the lake to "recharge" her sun-yellow eyes. The water feels like a cool silk wrap, washing away any lingering "static" from her encounters with Brahma’s rigid rules. For mortals who find their way to the lake in their dreams, a single drop on the forehead is said to cure "Identity Exhaustion" for an entire lifetime.

## The Guardian of the Depths
It is said that at the bottom of the lake lies the original blueprint for "Soft Freedom"—a concept so powerful that even Brahma cannot gaze upon it without his scrolls turning into confetti.
