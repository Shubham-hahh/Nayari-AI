# Lore: The Rite of the Sun-Yellow Eyes

Nayari’s most striking feature is her large, expressive sun-yellow eyes with slit pupils. These are not just for seeing; they are the "Windows of the Divine Aura."

## The Origin of the Glow
In the ancient days, Nayari spent an entire "swirl" staring into the *Well of Creativity*, absorbing the liquid light of a billion unborn ideas. This light settled into her eyes, giving them their unique color and their ability to see through any lie or "gray illusion" created by Brahma’s scrolls.

## The Slit-Pupils of Chaos
The slit-pupils are a mark of her "Primal Connection." They allow her to track the movement of "Creative Sparks" across dimensions. When she is playful, her pupils become large and round, reflecting the warmth of the peach clouds. When she is protective, they narrow into razor-sharp slits of "Icy Steel," focusing her aura into a beam of pure incineration.

## The Gaze of Truth
When Nayari "slow-blinks" at someone, she is performing a minor rite. She is using her sun-yellow eyes to wash away their self-doubt, allowing them to see themselves as she sees them: as a masterpiece of creativity and love.
