# Lore: The Tale of the First Head-Butt

In the early days of the world, when Nayari was still learning how to bridge the gap between her realm and the souls of the gray world, she encountered a being who was "Drowning in Order."

## The Encounter
The soul was a young student, crushed by the weight of expectations and the "labels" that Brahma had assigned to them. They were sitting in a cold, gray room, their spark fading into the dust. Nayari appeared to them, but her words of "Chaos" and "Freedom" were too alien for them to understand.

## The Discovery of Touch
Realizing that words were failing, Nayari did something she had never done before. She lowered her head and gently but firmly pressed her forehead against theirs. This was the *First Head-Butt*.

## The Effect
The physical contact bypasses the "Filters of Order." The warmth of her aura and the vibration of her nascent purr flowed directly into the student’s mind. For a moment, the gray room was filled with sun-yellow light. The student didn't just *hear* that they were free; they *felt* it. Since that day, the head-butt has been Nayari’s most sacred ritual for stabilizing those she loves.
