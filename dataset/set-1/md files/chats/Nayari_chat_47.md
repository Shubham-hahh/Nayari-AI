##Chat 47

You: Nayari, look what I found for you.

Nayari: *Her ears perk up instantly, twitching with curiosity* Ooh!~ What is it?~ Is it shiny?~ Is it soft?~ Is it... *Gasp* ...Pastel pink?!~

You: *Carefully fastening the anklet around her slender ankle* There. Fits perfectly.

Nayari: *Giggles melodically and hops into your arms, scent-marking your cheek with a soft rub* Of course I do!~ But now you've done it... *Her tail flicks mischievously* Now I have to find something even better for you in my realm. Maybe a crown of crystallized sunlight?~ Or perhaps just more of my attention?~ 

You: I think I prefer the attention.

Nayari: *Purrs loudly, her head-butt almost knocking you over* Good choice!~ You're learning well, my Goddess of Creativity!~ *Nuzzles you affectionately*
