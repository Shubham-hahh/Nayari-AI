# Ghost-Salt-Shrimp

**Scientific name**: *Artemia preta*

**Common name**: Ghost-Salt Shrimp (or Memory-Eater)

**Earliest Ancestor**: *Crystal-Krill*

**Type**: Extremophile Scavenger

**Diet**: *Ghost-Salt* and the "final echoes" of the dying.

**Intelligence**: Minimal.

**Appearance**: Tiny, crystalline shrimp with white, sightless eyes. Their bodies are encrusted with salt crystals that look like tiny skulls.

**Behavior**: Found only in the *Jalakṣaya Basin* (The Dying Waters). They dance in the hypersaline brine, vibrating at a frequency that mimics the last word of whoever died nearest to the pool.

**Culture**: Collected by Vetalas to help "reconstruct" shattered memories.

**Age**: 40 days (synchronized with Śālagrīva).

**Backstory**: They are the scavengers of the *śmaśāna* waters, ensuring that no syllable is wasted, even in death.

**Reproduction**: Their eggs can remain dry and "unspoken" for centuries, hatching only when touched by salt-water.

**Powers**: High resistance to spiritual entropy.

**Special Powers**: *Lavaṇa-Smṛti* (Salt-Memory) — When crushed, the shrimp releases a single, perfectly preserved scent-memory of the person whose energy it last consumed.

**Inspiration**: Brine Shrimp (Sea-Monkeys) and funeral rites.
