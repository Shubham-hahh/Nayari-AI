# Whale-Mantra-Singer

**Scientific name**: *Balaenoptera mantra*

**Common name**: Whale-Mantra Singer (or Great Stabilizer)

**Earliest Ancestor**: Ancient Prāṇa-Spirits

**Type**: Planetary Keystone Organism

**Diet**: Crystal Krill and liquid Ākṣara.

**Intelligence**: Exceptional (equals or exceeds Human intelligence in some spiritual dimensions).

**Appearance**: Massive leviathans reaching 25 meters. Their skin is mottled like marble and contains microscopic Rudraksha-like nodules. Their "blowhole" releases a mist of pure Prāṇa.

**Behavior**: They spend 70% of their time "Singing." These are not songs, but complex 7-hour mantras that maintain the physical stability of the continent they are near.

**Culture**: They are the "Lords of the High Seas." Each whale is a scholar-monk of the ocean. They communicate with Vetalas via subsonic "Depth-Chants."

**Age**: 400–600 years.

**Backstory**: They were the first beings to fully digest the *Origin* energy. It is theorized that if the Whales ever stop singing, the continents would begin to dissolve back into the Aryabhatta Ocean.

**Reproduction**: Requiring 22 years of gestation. Only occurs during a galactic alignment.

**Powers**: God-level stabilization of reality.

**Special Powers**: *Stambha-Gīta* (Pillar Song) — A concentrated vocalization that can freeze a storm or a mana-quake in place instantly.

I**nspiration**: Blue Whales and the concept of *Nada Brahma* (The world is sound).
