# Prana-Embodied-Squid

**Scientific name**: *Teuthida spiritualis*

**Common name**: Prāṇa-Squid (or Ghost-Tentacle)

**Earliest Ancestor**: Fragmented spirits of the *Universal Eclipse*

**Type**: Temporary Spirit-Host**Diet**: Syllabic sediment and spiritual echoes.

**Intelligence**: Variable (depends on the spirit inhabiting it).

**Appearance**: Translucent, gelatinous body that glows with a soft, pulsing golden light. They have ten tentacles that look like ribbons of smoke.

**Behavior**: They spend most of their life in the *Preta-loka* layer, surfacing only to release their spirit at the end of their life cycle.

**Culture**: None, but they are viewed as "Soul-Carriers" by the Vetalas.

**Age**: Exactly 40 days.

**Backstory**: They are not born; they "coalesce" when a fragment of unanchored Prāṇa spirit finds a shell of seawater and Ākṣara.

**Reproduction**: They do not reproduce. They dissolve into golden dust, which eventually reforms into new squids.

**Powers**: Can pass through solid objects (half-phased).

**Special Powers**: *Memory-Ink* — When threatened, they spray a cloud of "ink" that forces anyone who touches it to relive a random ancestor's memory.

**Inspiration**: Humboldt Squid and "Spirit" creatures in fantasy.
