# Ākṣara-Vent Flora

## The Chemosynthetic Scribes

**Scientific Classification:**

* **Kingdom:** Vanaspati (वनस्पति)
* **Phylum:** Tāpa-jīvī (तापजीवी) - Heat-Life
* **Class:** Ventophyceae (new class)
* **Order:** Ākṣarobacteriales (आक्षरोबक्टेरियलेस्)
* **Family:** Ventifloraceae (वेन्टिफ्लोरaceae)
* **Genus:** Ventiflora (वेन्टिफ्लोरा)
* **Species:** Ventiflora primordia (वेन्टिफ्लोरा प्रिमोर्डिया)

**Common Names:** Vent-Weed, Fire-Scribe, Mantra-Chimney, Core-Bloom

**Scientific Name**: Ventiflora primordia

**Normal Name:** Ākṣara-Vent Flora

**Morphological Description:** Chemosynthetic organisms forming 2.5m-tall crystalline "gardens" around hydrothermal vents. Structure consists of hollow stems (5-10cm diameter) with internal light channels conducting Ākṣara energy from vent effluent. No chlorophyll—energy derived from oxidation of Ākṣara compounds by symbiotic bacteria housed in stem chambers. Growth rings visible as alternating crystal-clear and violet-opaque bands, each representing 40-day lunar cycle. Fastest-growing known marine flora at 2.5 m/year under optimal conditions.

**Habitat & Distribution:** Restricted to 87 major Ākṣara-vent fields in abyssal zones (>2,000m). Concentrated in Sapta-Garta Trench region and Mid-Oceanic Mantra-Ridges. Requires Ākṣara concentrations >10 ppm and temperatures of 60-400°C from vent effluent. Forms exclusive communities with Vent-Worms (Riftia ākṣarica) in symbiotic "mantra-gardens."

**Ecological Role:** Primary producer in aphotic zones, supporting entire vent ecosystems. Light channels provide visual signals for deep-sea fauna and navigation markers for Vetala explorers. Crystal structures filter toxic vent compounds, converting them to stable Ākṣara forms. Stem decay releases mineralized Ākṣara that seeds abyssal plains. Production: 2,800 gC/m²/year (highest of any marine flora).

**Magical Properties:** Stems can be harvested as "eternal candles"—burn with violet flame that consumes no fuel, powered by residual Ākṣara oxidation. Gardens create localized reality-stable zones where Preta-Loka interference is nullified. Fluid extracted from stems provides "pure Ākṣara" for high-level spells, unmixed with surface impurities. Walking through gardens causes spontaneous mantra recitation—considered oracular experience.

**Population Estimate:** 0.8% of total marine flora biomass (extremely restricted distribution but hyper-abundant in vent fields)

**Economic Importance:** Crystalline stems valued at 500× weight in gold by Apsara jewelers. Vetala priests trade "vent-blessed water" for resurrection rituals. Kinnara shamans seek gardens for vision quests. Human kingdoms have attempted cultivation—all failed, causing reality-cracks. Collection requires death-pact with vent spirits (Vetala mediation essential).

**Danger:** Improper harvesting triggers vent instability, causing Ākṣara eruptions that can crystallize entire valleys. Only Vetalas know the silencing mantras to safely collect specimens.

**Etymology:** Latin "ventus" (wind) + "flora" + "primordia" (first beginnings), referencing vent origins and primordial energy processing.
