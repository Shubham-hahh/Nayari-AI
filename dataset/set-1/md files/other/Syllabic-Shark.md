# Syllabic-Shark

**Scientific name**: *Carcharhinus phonemicus*

**Common name**: Syllabic Shark (or The Silent Listener)

**Earliest Ancestor**: Unknown (Deep-Sea origin)

**Type**: Apex Mnemonic Predator

**Diet**: Marine mammals and large fish.

**Intelligence**: High (tactical and patient).

**Appearance**: A 6-meter shark with skin the texture of rough slate. They have no eyes; instead, they have specialized "Ear-Slits" along their snout that detect the vibration of cellular Ākṣara.

**Behavior**: They hunt in total silence. They do not track by smell or sight, but by "hearing" the name/identity of their prey.

**Culture**: Feared as the "Judges of the Deep." It is said they only eat those whose personal mantra has become "distorted" by lies.

**Age**: 150–200 years.

**Backstory**: Emerged from the *Sapta-Garta Trench*. They are believed to be the guardians of the planet's "Memory Integrity," culling lifeforms that have become spiritually unstable.

**Reproduction**: Live birth. Pups are born in the *Preta-loka* hour and must immediately learn to hide their own "sound" to survive.

**Powers**: Exceptional sensory perception of spiritual resonances.

**Special Powers**: *Silence-Field* — They can project a 15-meter zone where no sound or magic can be produced, rendering prey defenseless.

I**nspiration**: Great White Sharks and the concept of "The Silence" in mysticism.
