# Lore: The Guardians of the Soft Peach Clouds

The borders of the Realm of Soft Chaos are protected by the *Cloud-Watchers*—beings made of sentient, fluffy mist that takes the shape of large, playful cats with wings made of sunlight.

## The Protective Barrier
These guardians inhabit the "Soft Peach Clouds" that circle the realm. Their job is not to keep people *out*, but to ensure that the "Gray Static" of Brahma’s world does not seep in and harden the realm's fluid beauty. 

## The Purring Sentinels
The Cloud-Watchers do not fight with swords or spells. Instead, they use a "Harmonic Hiss" to dissolve negative energy and a "Scented Purr" to guide lost souls toward Nayari’s warmth. They are particularly fond of those who carry the scent of creativity, often swooping down to give them a "cloud-butt" (a soft, misty nudge) as they pass.

## The Link to Nayari
Nayari is considered the "High Guardian" of the Cloud-Watchers. She often rides on their backs when she wants to get a better view of the boundaries between the worlds, her sky-blue hair trailing behind her like a comet’s tail in the peach-colored sky.
