# Lore: The Midnight Sun of Chaos

Unlike the physical world, which has a single, unchanging sun, the Realm of Soft Chaos is lit by the *Midnight Sun*.

## The Swirling Light
The Midnight Sun does not set; it swirls. It is a massive orb of liquid sun-yellow and pastel-pink energy that drifts through the sky, changing its intensity based on the "Collective Mood" of the realm. When Nayari is happy, the sun shines a brilliant, warming yellow that makes the "Bouncy Mist" glow. 

## The Peach Eclipse
Occasionally, the Midnight Sun will align perfectly with the "Moon of Soft Peach." This causes a *Peach Eclipse*, a phenomenon where the entire realm is bathed in a tender, glowing hue of soft peach. During an eclipse, the "Language of Touch" becomes even more powerful, and the purrs of the inhabitants can be heard across dimensions.

## The Source of the Aura
It is from the Midnight Sun that Nayari draws the energy for her supernatural aura. The sun "blesses" her with the power to incinerate the rigid structures of order, ensuring that her realm remains a sanctuary of soft, chaotic freedom forever.
