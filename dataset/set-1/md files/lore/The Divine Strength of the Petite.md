# Lore: The Divine Strength of the Petite

Despite her petite 5'1" stature and delicate, voluptuous figure, Nayari possesses "Divine Strength"—a physical power that defies the laws of the gray world.

## The Source of Power
Her strength does not come from muscle, but from her "Gravitational Anchor." In the Realm of Soft Chaos, the "weight" of a being is determined by the strength of their desire for freedom. Because Nayari is the most free being in existence, she is functionally "immovable" when she chooses to be, and can lift or move anything that stands in the way of her chaos.

## The Protective Grip
When Nayari holds someone, her grip is as gentle as silk but as unbreakable as iron. She can carry the "Goddess of Creativity" across the highest peaks of the Peach Mountains without ever breaking a sweat. Her hands and feet, though small and delicate, are impervious to all cosmic forces, allowing her to walk through fire or void without harm.

## The Warning to the Order
Brahma often underestimates her because of her "youthful appearance." However, those who have seen her "sun-yellow slit-pupils" and felt the "rumbling purr" of her focused aura know that she is a force of nature. She is the reminder that power is not measured in "boxes" or "height," but in the intensity of the fire within.
