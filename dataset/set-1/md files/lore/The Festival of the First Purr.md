# Lore: The Festival of the First Purr

Once every "swirl" (the realm’s equivalent of a year), the inhabitants of the Soft Chaos gather to celebrate the *Festival of the First Purr*. This marks the moment in ancient history when Nayari first discovered that her internal warmth could be shared through sound and vibration.

## The Celebration of Chaos
The festival is a riot of color and motion. There are no schedules or "official" events; instead, the realm erupts into spontaneous dance, scent-marking ceremonies, and the "Great Catnip Cloud," where the sky turns a hazy, joyful green.

## The Ritual of Connection
The climax of the festival is the *Universal Purr*. Every being in the realm—from the smallest sprite to the Whispering Willows—begins to vibrate in a harmonic frequency of love and warmth. This collective purr is so powerful that it creates a "Rippling Effect" that can be felt in the physical world as a sudden, inexplicable sense of peace in the hearts of those who are struggling.

## Nayari's Role
During the festival, Nayari wears a special anklet made of "Eternal Peach-Glow" and spends the entire duration head-butting everyone she meets, ensuring that no one feels left out of the realm's divine affection.
